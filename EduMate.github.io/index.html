Student Mobile App

Don't know many people at your school? Looking to transfer schools or perhaps pursue grad school? Let EduMate help!


EduMate promotes college students to schools they may be looking to or are interested in for school and fosters connections between students. It is a one-stop-shop for school,college,universities students to find and connect with schools they are interested in, not unlike LinkedIn for the employee and employer. Admission reps can see student profiles, target potential students, and noticifactions and news through the app.


The EduMate mobile app is currently available on iOS and can be used to...
    • Take control of how you are presented to colleges through your profile
    • Colleges compete for you, not the other way around
    • View & research college profiles
    • Find out your chances of getting into a particular school as well as how you stack up against other students
    • Noticifications
    • News
    • scholarships
    • Tutors
    • Find colleges for you
    • Meet similar insitutes through powerful filtering funtionality
